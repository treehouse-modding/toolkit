# Plugins

## Mixing

* [OTT](https://splice.com/plugins/3788-ott-vst-au-by-xfer-records): A powerful compressor from **Xfer**. (free)
* [Ozone Imager](https://www.izotope.com/en/products/ozone-imager): A stereo imaging tool that can subtly widen your mix and enhance spatial depth, especially useful during mastering. (paid)
* [FabFilter Series](https://www.fabfilter.com/): A high-quality suite of plugins including **EQs**, **compressors**, **limiters**, and **more**. Known for their clean sound, intuitive interfaces, and advanced control compared to stock **Fruity** plugins. (paid)

## Synthesizers

* [daHornet](https://nusofting.net/plugins/dahornet-bis-au-vst3-2024-synthesizer-plugin): A synthesizer which has a few sounds that were used in the **Terraria** OST. (paid)
* [chipsounds](https://www.plogue.com/products/chipsounds.html): Although I don't think **Terraria** used any sounds from this plugin, it has a lot of sounds that fit the overall vibe of "Vanilla Style" music. (paid)
* [Albino 3](https://www.robpapen.com/albino-3.html): **Albino 3** is yet another synthesizer which also has a few sounds that were used in the **Terraria** OST. (paid)
